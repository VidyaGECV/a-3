MY VILLAGE


My village is nestled between rolling hills and lush green fields. A crystal-clear river flows through the heart of our community, providing water for our crops and a playground for children during hot summer days. The water glistens in the sunlight,
creating small rainbows that dance on its surface. Tall, ancient trees form a protective canopy around our homes.
Some of these trees are older than our oldest residents and have witnessed generations grow up under their branches. During spring, wildflowers carpet the meadows in vibrant colors—purples, yellows, and whites creating a natural garden that no human hand could design better. The changing seasons bring different kinds of beauty to our village. Summer brings rich greenery and abundant fruits, autumn transforms the landscape into golden and red hues, winter covers everything in a peaceful blanket of snow, and spring awakens the earth with new life and fresh blooms

Village Life and Daily Routines


Life in my village follows the rhythm of the sun. Most villagers wake up early with the roosters’ call. Farmers head to their fields, tending to crops and animals before the day gets too hot. Women prepare breakfast, often using fresh ingredients from their kitchen gardens. Children walk to the small village school together, carrying their books and sharing stories along the way. Our school building is simple but filled with eager minds and dedicated teachers who know each student by name. In the evenings, after the day’s work is done, people often gather in the village square. Elders sit on wooden benches, sharing wisdom and stories from the past while children play traditional games nearby. These moments of togetherness strengthen our community bonds.
